# FlowaeyProject

Flowaey is a free service that helps customers keep track of their mail when they move.
With Flowaey, you can manage all your subscriptions in one place to make sure your mail
is sent to your new adress. A bunch of related services are also offered to help you 
change your phone number and alert all your contacts or just to manage your monthly 
subscriptions.
